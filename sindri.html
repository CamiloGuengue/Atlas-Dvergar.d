Its origin is a very sad one. The issue is his past with his brother, as he was the only family he had.
On his journey alone without his family, he travels the universe in search of a purpose, and during his travels he meets four dwarves from different parts of the vast universe.
Gunnar was the first dwarf to strike up a conversation. From the moment they spotted each other, they set off together in search of a purpose, sharing knowledge and stories along the way.
The second dwarf they found was Thorin. They saw that he was a man of great principles and also a great leader when it came to guiding others, demonstrating courage and a great sense of loyalty.
As the last dwarf, they met Bofur, a dwarf born of mining and a great master of minerals.
So the four set off, and each one naturally found a family and support in the others, sharing their knowledge and stories lived and shared on their long journeys.
